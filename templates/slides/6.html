Experimental Testing Setup

To create our initial DPLA testing record set, we took JSON records from three DPLA collections from the January 2016 bulk downloads, extracted just the sourceResource from the JSON to create a simplified graph that was added to the testing record set. These collections are. :

  • University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (https://dpla-provider-export.s3.amazonaws.com/2016/01/uiuc.json.gz), total JSON records of 18,231. This collection was chosen to represent a smaller collection and I confess as because of a personal connection to this institution (GSLIS 2003 graduate)
  • The Getty (https://dpla-provider-export.s3.amazonaws.com/2016/01/getty.json.gz) as mid-range collection .
  • Smithsonian (https://dpla-provider-export.s3.amazonaws.com/2016/01/smithsonian.json.gz) collection as the largest single.
